Upload a .fnmm file and convert it to a V-Slice chart JSON ready to use in your mod.

📖 How to Use
▼
📦
Export from FNMobile Export your chart as a .fnmm file from the FNMobile chart editor.
📂
Upload the file Drag & drop or click to browse for your .fnmm file in Step 1.
⚙️
Set chart options Fill in song title, difficulty, scroll speed, timing offset, and lane mode.
⬇️
Download the JSON Click Convert then download your V-Slice songname-chart.json file.
💡 Tip: Place your downloaded JSON in your mod's data/songs/songname/ folder. The file must be named exactly songname-chart.json to be recognised by V-Slice. Player vs. opponent assignment is detected automatically from the scene.json inside your .fnmm file.
